Final Weight Prediction Model

Predict your weight change based on various health and lifestyle factors.

Input Parameters

Enter the following details to get an estimate of your predicted weight:

  • Age: Your age in years.
  • Gender: Specify if you are Male, Female, etc.
  • Current Weight: Your current weight in kilograms (kg).
  • BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate): Estimated rate of energy expenditure while at rest (in kcal).
  • Daily Calories Consumed: Average daily calorie intake in kcal.
  • Caloric Surplus or Deficit: Caloric difference based on intake and expenditure.
  • Weight Change: Any observed weight changes over time (in kg).
  • Duration: The duration of time (in days) over which weight change is tracked.
  • Physical Activity Level: A rating of your daily activity level.
  • Sleep Quality: Your subjective rating of sleep quality.
  • Stress Level: Your current stress level.
